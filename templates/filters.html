25/26 Fixtures - Mapped

This tool lets you browse and map football fixtures from the 25/26 season across leagues and countries.

Use the filters on the left to narrow results by country, league, or team, then click Apply filters to see fixtures plotted on the map.

You can hover over any row to highlight its stadium on the map, or click a row to zoom in and open its popup.

Markers are color-coded by fixture date; see the legend in the bottom-right corner.

Click the “i” button any time to revisit this page.
